# **App Name**: Circuit Vision

## Core Features:

- Race Simulation: Simulate an F1 race with 20 cars, considering weather conditions and pit stops. Uses real-world data for accurate physics.
- Dynamic Track Visualization: Display a dynamic track with cars as dots, updating in real-time to reflect their positions, speed and the location of DRS zones.
- Leaderboard with Real-time Updates: Show a leaderboard that highlights overtaking cars in real-time.
- AI Pit Stop Strategy Tool: Use AI tool to suggest optimal pit stop strategies based on race conditions, tire wear, and competitor activity.
- AI-Powered Commentary Generation: Provide real-time AI-generated commentary about overtakes and leaderboard changes
- Weather Condition Simulation: Accurately simulate changing weather conditions (dry, wet, intermediate) and their impact on car performance.
- Driver Detail Modal: Clicking on any driver on the leaderboard will show the drivers name and give you an ability to make prop bets.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Racing Green (#3B5A37) evokes the tradition and thrill of racing.
- Background color: Dark Gray (#222222) provides a modern dark mode interface.
- Accent color: Electric Yellow (#FFFF33) will highlight real-time updates and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif with a machined modern feel.
- Use sleek, minimalist icons to represent speed, weather conditions, and other key race data.
- Responsive layout adapting to different screen sizes for optimal viewing experience.
- Subtle transitions and animations to highlight overtakes, pit stops, and other race events.
